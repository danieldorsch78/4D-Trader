## Full Description (4000 chars max)

4D Market Intelligence is a professional-grade, cross-market analysis cockpit for Android. Monitor and analyze DAX, Brazilian B3, cryptocurrency, and commodity markets — all from one clean, dark terminal interface.

★ MULTI-MARKET WATCHLISTS
Track DAX 30, Ibovespa/B3, Gold, Silver, Oil, Bitcoin, Ethereum, and more. Create unlimited custom watchlists. Real-time quotes with bid/ask spreads, volume, and data quality indicators.

★ AI SIGNAL CENTER
Get rule-based technical analysis with full transparency. Every signal includes RSI, MACD, SMA/EMA crossovers, ATR, and Bollinger Bands — plus confidence scoring and step-by-step reasoning so you understand WHY a signal fires.

★ CROSS-ASSET CORRELATION ENGINE
Discover hidden relationships between markets. Pearson & Spearman correlation over 30/60/90/120-day rolling windows. 11+ predefined pairs including DAX↔Gold, DAX↔Bitcoin, Ibovespa↔Oil, Petrobras↔Oil, Vale↔Gold, and more. Stability assessment tells you if correlations are holding or breaking.

★ WORLD MARKET CLOCK
Live clocks for Berlin, São Paulo, New York, and UTC. See which exchanges are open right now — Xetra, B3, NYSE, NASDAQ, CME, COMEX, NYMEX, LSE, and 24/7 crypto markets.

★ SMART ALERTS
Set alerts for price targets, percentage moves, correlation thresholds, volatility spikes, signal confidence levels, and market open/close events. Full event history with snooze and dismiss.

★ PREDICTION ENGINE
Multi-timeframe trend analysis combining technical indicators into actionable trade setups with entry, target, and stop-loss levels. Composite scoring from Strong Buy to Strong Sell.

★ PROFESSIONAL DARK UI
Terminal-style dark theme optimized for Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra and all modern Android devices. Clean, information-dense layouts designed for fast decision-making.

★ YOUR DATA, YOUR DEVICE
No account required. No tracking. No ads. All data stays on your device. API keys are stored encrypted. Uses free market data APIs — no subscription needed.

★ FREE DATA SOURCES
Works out-of-the-box with CoinGecko (crypto), and optionally with free API keys from Finnhub (stocks), Alpha Vantage (historical data), and Brapi (Brazilian markets).

DISCLAIMER: 4D Market Intelligence is a decision-support and educational tool. It does NOT provide financial advice, investment recommendations, or trading signals. All analysis is based on historical data and statistical models — past performance does not guarantee future results.

Developed by Daniel Dorsch — 4D Digital Solutions, Berlin.
